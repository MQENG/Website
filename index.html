About Us

The Macquarie Engineering Student Society (EngSoc) is a vibrant student-run organization dedicated to supporting and uniting engineering students at Macquarie University.

Through industry connections, social events, and professional development programs, we provide opportunities for students to develop their skills and networks. Join us to expand your horizons beyond the classroom.
